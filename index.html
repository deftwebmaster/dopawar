Philosophy
Not war
against self.
War against
engineered
drift.

Most systems blame you. They frame compulsion as weakness, relapse as failure, drift as character. That frame is wrong — and it's profitable for someone else that you believe it.

Attention is harvested. Environments are optimized against your baseline. Every feed, every interface, every default setting was designed to keep you in a loop that costs you and pays someone else.

The counter-move isn't willpower. It's architecture. Design your environment, name the loop, interrupt the trigger, replace the reward. No shame. No surrender. Systems.
